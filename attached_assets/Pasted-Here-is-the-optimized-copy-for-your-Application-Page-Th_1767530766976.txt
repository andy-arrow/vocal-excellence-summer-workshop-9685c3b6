### FAQ Section (Rewrite)

**Header:**
**Frequently Asked Questions**

**Q: Do I need formal conservatory training to apply?**
**A:** Not necessarily. While many of our students have formal backgrounds, we prioritize potential, passion, and a willingness to learn. If you are serious about improving your voice, we want to hear from you.

**Q: Is accommodation included?**
**A:** Tuition covers all training and daily lunch, but accommodation is not included. We can provide a list of recommended hotels and Airbnbs near the venue in Limassol upon acceptance.

**Q: What happens after I submit my application?**
**A:** You will receive a confirmation email immediately. Our faculty reviews applications on a rolling basis, with final decisions released by June 1, 2026.

**Q: Do I need to submit a recommendation letter?**
**A:** No, recommendation letters are not required for this workshop.

**Q: Is there financial aid available?**
**A:** We offer a flexible payment plan (pay in 3 installments) and an Early Bird discount if you register before March 1, 2026.

**Q: Can I attend only part of the workshop?**
**A:** Because the curriculum is designed as a cumulative, intensive process leading to a final performance, full attendance is required to get the most out of the experience.
